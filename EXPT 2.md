
## CHARACTERISTICS OF THERMISTOR
# AIM
To study the characteristics of Thermistor.

# APPARATUS REQUIRED 

1. Thermistor 1 Nos
2. Hot bath 1 Nos
3. Thermometer
4. Multimeter
   
# EXPERIMENTAL SETUP

# THEORY
Thermistors are Sensors used to measure temperature by correlating the resistance of the
element. Thermistors are characterized by linear negative change in resistance with respect to
temperature. They exhibit the most linear signal with respect to temperature of any electronic sensing
device.

# PROCEDURE
1. Fill the hot bath with water until the level the thermometer bulb and sensor end is completely
immersed.
2. Connect the terminal ends to the multimeter terminals.
3. Heat the water till it boils at 100 deg C.
4. Switch off the water bath and allow the system to cool.
5. During the process of colling tabulate resistance values corresponding to the respective
temperature.
6. Plot the graph and determine the sensitivity.
7. Tabulate readings and plot graph to study characteristics;
i)Resistance-Temperature for thermistor
